Highlights

JavaScript
is the most popular primary language among local developers, followed by Python and HTML/CSS.

Most developers consider themselves to be a full stack developer.

The top 5 languages people want to use this year (2024) are JavaScript, Java, HTML/CSS, C#, and Python.

Among developers who have experience with higher academics, 47.3% studied Information Technology.

Tacloban City is where most of our developers are located in. Most of them consider the city to be their hometown.

UP Tacloban has been reported to provide the most learning satisfaction among those who proceeded to higher education.

26% of professional/freelancer participants reported they have annual income range of PHP 10,000 - PHP 100,000.

Most developers take a neutral stance on rising issues involving artificial intelligence, namely on training data sets and AI art.

Technologies

Primary Languages

More than half of developers consider JavaScript as their primary language regardless of occupation. The next two top languages are Python and HTML/CSS. Among professionals and freelancers, however, both JavaScript and Python share the top spot.

It comes to no surprise that the top five primary languages for professionals, JavaScript, Python, PHP, HTML/CSS, and TypeScript, are usually associated with the web. This is reflective of the fact that most of our developers develop websites.

Students almost share the same top five languages, but with TypeScript being replaced with Java. We believe that this is most likely due to the current educational curriculum where Java is a part of, particularly for courses on the object-oriented paradigm.
