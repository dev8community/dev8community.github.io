Highlights

JavaScript is the most popular primary language among local developers, followed by Python and HTML/CSS.

Most developers consider themselves to be a full stack developer.

The top 5 languages people want to use this year (2024) are JavaScript, Java, HTML/CSS, C#, and Python.

Among developers who have experience with higher academics, 47.3% studied Information Technology, followed by Computer Science at 41.9%.

Tacloban City is where most of our developers are located in. Most of them consider the city to be their hometown, with some hailing from other places in the province of Leyte.

UP Tacloban has been reported to provide the most learning satisfaction among those who proceeded to higher education. This is followed, in alphabetical order, by ADFC, ESSU, SSU, and VSU.

26% of professional/freelancer participants reported they have annual income range of PHP 10,000 - PHP 100,000. Most report they feel neutral or dissatisfied with their income. It must be noted that 19% of professionals/freelancers prefer not to share their annual income, while 17.4% earn between PHP 250,000 and PHP 400,000.

Most developers take a neutral stance on rising issues on AI. Most are neutral on the debate on the ethics behind the data sets used for training AI models, and whether AI art can be considered art and if such works can be copyrighted. Interestingly, most report to be neutral or agree that AI developer tools can be trained on open source repositories.
