Highlights

JavaScript
is the most popular primary language among local developers, followed by Python and HTML/CSS.

Most developers consider themselves to be full stack developers.

The top 5 languages people want to use this year (2024) are JavaScript, Java, HTML/CSS, C#, and Python.

Tacloban City is where most of our developers are located in. Most of them consider the city to be their hometown.

UP Tacloban has been reported to provide the most learning satisfaction among those who proceeded to higher education.

20.6% of professional/freelancer participants reported they have an annual income range of PHP 10,000 - PHP 100,000.

Most developers take a neutral stance on rising issues involving artificial intelligence, namely on training data sets and AI art.
